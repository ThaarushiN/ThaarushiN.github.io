At **Global Rubber Industries**, I engineered an internal **document analysis and conversational AI system** integrating **Azure Document Intelligence** with **Azure OpenAI Service**.

**Key Contributions:**
- Built an **LLM-powered chatbot** capable of parsing and answering queries from uploaded corporate documents.  
- Designed a **FastAPI backend** with multi-threaded request handling to improve throughput and reliability.  
- Implemented **load testing** with Locust and Redis task queues to ensure high concurrency and stable response times.  
- Integrated with the company’s Azure environment for **secure and scalable deployment**.

**Impact:**  
Empowered employees to retrieve critical operational data through natural language queries, significantly improving internal knowledge accessibility and productivity.
