# Winning Super AI's NEXT Hackathon 2026

On the 8th of June 2026, our team at sixn.ai flew to Singapore all the way from Colombo to participate in NEXT Hackathon 2026. This year's hackathon theme was Agentic AI applications. Our project was **Bloom**, a multi-agent fashion companion that helps you build your dream wardrobe.

I am happy to announce that our team has become one of five overall winners and also won the category **Best Use of Stripe**.
